Parent Information

Academics


Brothers must maintain at least a 2.500 grade point ratio each semester. Members who fall below this level are placed on academic probation for one semester until the GPA is improved. While on probation they cannot attend socials or meetings.

Hazing


"Hazing" is not allowed, and is in no way/shape/form endorsed by our chapter or national organization. At no time during pledge-ship is any form of hazing endorsed or allowed.

Financial


There are certain financial requirements, including social dues, national and province fees, and housing/meal plans that are necessary to keep the chapter functioning. We use a financial management company called Omega Financial which will present you as a parent with a bill prior to each semester, outlining all of the costs.
